Charity compass is a non-profit website conceptualised and developed for the sake of the needy. Starting off as a university project in Morocco, Charity Compass made its way into the hearts of donors all around the world by providing ease of use, security and efficacy to the world of benevolence. We rallied people all around the globe for one purpose: To help.
At charity compass, we aspire to bring about the change we want to see in the world. We guide kind hands all around the world towards the betterment of the globe, through communication and collaboration. We hope to alleviate the suffering of the needy and give purpose to those in search for it. We make helping an easy task by locating, guiding and informing about the different crises in the world; Join us now, every little bit counts! Make the world a better place in your own way.
With Charity Compass, we hope to lead by example. The world doesn't need to be a dark place, so we'll be the first torch of many. We will lead the way to help those in need, and rally those that can help. Together, with volunteers, donators, and developers alike, we will make our world a place of betterment and cooperation, bringing about the good in people and shining light on the matters that need to be adressed most.
